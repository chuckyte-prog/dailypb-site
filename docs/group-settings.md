# Group Settings

Groups are a very powerful feature within DailyPB. Here's how to get the most out of it.

# General Settings

## Main image

This will be displayed large when users come to your page. It will also be part of a rich snippets when you send your Group link via popular social platforms like Facebook and WhatsApp. 

## Short Description

Very short 240 characters max. This will be displayed on the Home tab. This section may also be picked up via rich snippets when you are copying the group URL to Facebook, WhatsApp, or other social media sites.  It is also considered the description for SEO purposes. 

![Example of a rich snippet on WhatsApp ](/img/notion-help/group-settings/image.png)

Example of a rich snippet on WhatsApp 

## Group Name

Just the name of your group.

## Subheadline

This will go in the same section as your group name on your group home page.

## Group Location

This is very important when users are looking at groups to join. DailyPB will use this location to display all Groups within 50 miles of the user's location. 

![image.png](/img/notion-help/group-settings/image-1.png)

## Primary venue

If you have a public Group, then your Group will be displayed on this Venue's page at the top. 

![image.png](/img/notion-help/group-settings/image-2.png)

## Website URL

Your group page will get its own unique URL that you can and share. Each URL starts with [dailypb.com/group/](http://dailypb.com/group/)[yourcustomtext].  What you enter here will be your custom text.  

Note that this is the only field you cannot change once you set it without messaging DailyPB help. Also you don't want to change this frequently. 

# Community Settings

## Group Privacy

You can choose private or public. Public groups are available for any user to find or search. Private groups are only available through your unique group URL 

## Connected Interest Lists

Connected Interest Lists will be displayed on your group home page. This is a great way to quickly allow users to sign up for your Interest List 

## Social Media and Websites

This area allows you to link up any affiliated websites, Instagram profiles, Facebook groups, Facebook community chats, or WhatsApp groups and have them ready as links on your Group home page. 

# Members Mangement

## Membership Options

The three options are pretty self-explanatory. These can be changed at any time. 

## Welcome Email

Here you can select the email will be sent automatically when someone joins the Group on their own. It won't be sent if a Group admin adds them manually.

Templates listed here include ones you're an Admin of and any assigned to this Group.

Manage Templates on your Host page: [https://dailypb.com/host](https://dailypb.com/host)

# Sessions Settings

## Max Sessions to Show on Main Page

 You can specify how many sessions to show in your upcoming sessions section of the main group page.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/group-settings/image-3.png)

## Auto-promote Session

When turned on, the next upcoming Sessions (linked to this Group) with available spots will automatically appear at the top of your Group page.

When turned off, you can to manually select which upcoming Session to feature.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/group-settings/image-4.png)

## Promoted Session

This will be available if you do not have auto-promote sessions turned on. 

Here you can specify exactly which session will be highlighted at the top of your Group page for extra visibility. It automatically disappears once the session ends—no need to remove it manually.

You can choose any Session linked to this Group that starts in the next two months.

# Media

This section allows you to load images to display in the photos tab of your Group.
