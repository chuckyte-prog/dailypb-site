# 2 & 24 Hr Reminders

<aside>
🔔

Automated reminders sent 24 hours and 2 hours before a session starts significantly reduce no-shows and late cancellations.

</aside>

## For Attendees

### 24 Hour Reminder

Attendees receive a notification with their session start time and a direct link for additional information.

### 2 Hour Reminder

Attendees receive a final notification with their session start time and a direct link for additional information.

## For Waitlisters (24-hour Reminder)

This message reminds them that they’re still on the waitlist and includes a **one-click link to remove themselves** if they know they can’t attend or have made other plans. This keeps the Waitlist accurate and active.

If a spot opens up, the system checks with the first person on the waitlist and **waits for them to cancel** before moving to the next player. Because Waitlisters were already prompted to opt out when they can’t make it, confirmations happen faster and the Session fills more smoothly—cutting down on last-minute no-shows and stalled waitlists.

## For Hosts

### 24 Hour Reminder Email Includes

1. Session Info: name, date, time, location, link to session
2. List of all Attendees: name, email, and phone number
3. Total number of: Signups, Waitlisted, Spots available
4. Total number of 24-hour reminders sent via email and text

### 2 Hour Reminder Email Includes

A summary of which attendees received 2-hour reminders and whether they were sent by email or text.
