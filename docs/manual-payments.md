# Manual Payments

### Overview

Manual Payments allows hosts to use popular payment methods like Venmo, CashApp, Zelle, PayPal, or cash. 

Here's a comprehensive guide to get you started and help you understand everything Manual Payments has to offer. This provides you with immediate access to funds through channels that you and your participants may already be comfortable using.

### Why Use Manual Payments?

- **Instant Payouts**: Receive payments directly from participants as soon as they pay.
- **Lower Fees**: The fee for Manual Payments is only **5% per transaction** (minimum $1), compared to 7.9% + $0.30 with Automatic Payments.
- **No Stripe Account Required**: Manual Payments are perfect if you prefer not to set up a Stripe account.
- **Full Pro Feature Access**: Use features like Reminders, Text Messaging, Rosters, Round Robins, Interest Lists, and more with Manual Payments enabled. (Accessed features subject to change at any time).
- **Track Payments Easily**: Manually mark participants as paid within the DailyPB platform so you and your players know who has completed their payments.
- **Reach Out to Unpaid Participants**: You can easily bulk-message unpaid participants directly through DailyPB to remind them to pay.

### How Does DailyPB Collect Manual Payment Fees

When using Manual Payments, the DailyPB service fee is already included in the price shown to players before they sign up. Hosts collect the full payment directly from players using their chosen payment methods (e.g., Venmo, PayPal, Cash). DailyPB tracks these signups and automatically charges the host’s credit card on file at the end of each month for the total accumulated DailyPB fees.

### How to Set Up Manual Payments

You will need a Payment Method on file with DailyPB in order to use Manual Payments. This is the payment method that is charged for the fees (5% of Host fee with $0.50 minimum per Signup).

1. **Navigate to Monetization Settings in your Profile**: Go to your account settings and select the "Payments" tab.
2. **Choose Manual Payments**: Select "Manual Payments" from the payment options list.
3. **Add Payment Method**: Select “Add Payment Method” to save a payment method to DailyPB. This is what DailyPB will use to charge the 5% fees each month.

4. **Optional**: you can also save your alternative payment IDs (ie: Venmo) which will then save you time when making your Events in the future.

### Receiving Payments

- When participants sign up for your session, they will see your preferred payment methods and instructions.
- Participants will make the payment directly via the platform of their choice (e.g., Venmo).
- Once payment is received, you will be able to manually mark that participant as "Paid" on DailyPB.

### Marking Participants as Paid

- **Access the Attendees Screen**: Go to the Session page and click to view all participants.
- **Mark as Paid**: Next to each participant's name, you will see a "Mark as Paid" button. Click this once payment is confirmed.
- **Visual Confirmation**: Participants marked as paid will have a visible indicator, so you and they both know the payment is complete.

### Reaching Out to Unpaid Participants

- **Automatic Notifications**: DailyPB will remind participants to pay upon signup.
- **Direct Messages**: You can send reminders manually to unpaid participants by clicking the text messaging option on the attendees screen. There is an option to text all “unpaids.
- **Track Payment Status**: Use the participant list to easily see the status of each payment and identify who needs a reminder.

## Fees for Manual Payments

The Main Host (Host in first position) will be assessed DailyPB fees based on the number of Attendees in the Session at the time the Session starts. Manually marking Attendees as Paid or Unpaid has no bearing on fees.

- **5% per Signup X Event Fee**: A fee of **5% of the Event Fee per Signup** is charged for using Manual Payments. There is a minimum fee of **$1 per Signup**.
- **End of Month Billing**: Fees are charged at the end of the month if your total balance exceeds **$10**. Ensure you have a payment method on file to handle these fees.

### Flexible and Fair

- **Session Cancellation:** If a session is deleted or cancelled before it starts, no fees will apply.
- **Player Cancellations:** Players who cancel before the start of the Session will not be included in the fee calculation.
- **No Subscription Fees**: Only pay for what you use.
- **Financial Dashboard**: access your financial dashboard at [dailypb.com/host](http://dailypb.com/host) and click “Finance”

### Benefits vs. Automatic Payments

- **Lower Fees**: With Manual Payments, players pay only **5% of the Event Fee per Signup** compared to the higher rate of Automatic Payments.
- **Direct Payment Flexibility**: Payments are made directly to you, which means immediate access to funds without waiting for a payment processor.
- **No Payment Integration Required**: If you prefer not to integrate with Stripe, Manual Payments is the perfect option, giving you the flexibility to use your preferred payment platforms.

### Considerations for Manual Payments

- **Manual Tracking**: Optional manual tracking requires you (or another host) to manually mark each participant as paid, which can be more time-consuming than Automatic Payments.
- **Participant Compliance**: Participants may forget to complete payments after signing up, so occasional reminders may be necessary to ensure full payment compliance.
- **Payment Channels**: Make sure participants are comfortable using the payment options you provide (e.g., Venmo, PayPal).

### Common Questions

**1. Can I switch between Manual and Automatic Payments?**

- Yes, you can switch payment methods at any time by editing the settings on your Session.

**2. What happens if someone doesn't pay?**

- You can follow up with unpaid participants using DailyPB's messaging feature. If payment isn’t received, you may choose to cancel their registration for the session. If you cancel their participation before the start of the session, DailyPB will not assess the 5% charge for their signup.

**3. Is there any limit on the payment methods I can use?**

- You can add multiple options (Venmo, CashApp, Zelle, PayPal, cash) to ensure participants have flexibility when making payments.

### Summary

Manual Payments offer a flexible, cost-effective way to collect fees directly from participants, with lower transaction fees and no Stripe setup required. However, they do require manual effort if tracking payments is important to you. If you’re looking for complete control over your payment process and prefer direct transfers, Manual Payments are the perfect solution for you.
