# How DailyPB Advertises Your Event

Every event you add to DailyPB is instantly visible to the 250+ players who check the schedule each day. Events with signups get an extra boost—promoted across the platform and direct via social media to nearly 100,000 local views each month, all for free.

<aside>
⚠️

Everything in this guide applies to sessions that use **DailyPB signups**.

</aside>

## Main Schedule Page (index)

### Featured Sessions

This section displays paid ads first, followed by available public sessions with spots still open, sorted by date (up to 14 days out) within the user’s area. On mobile, only the first session appears initially, with more available by scrolling right. 

![image.png](/img/notion-help/how-dailypb-advertises-your-event/image.png)

### **Daily Schedule**

This popular section displays the day’s schedule, with public sessions offering signups highlighted at the top.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/how-dailypb-advertises-your-event/image-1.png)

### Signup Section

This section, accessed via a main button or social media link, lists all public sessions with signups enabled in the user’s preferred area. 

![image.png](/img/notion-help/how-dailypb-advertises-your-event/image-2.png)

## Session Page

At the bottom of each Session page, users see ‘Other Events From You,’ showing your next 8 public sessions with open spots so they can quickly sign up.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/how-dailypb-advertises-your-event/image-3.png)

## Venue Page

Below the main schedule on a Venue page, the ‘Nearby Events You Might Like’ section lists sessions with signups at that venue first, then sessions within 15 miles.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/how-dailypb-advertises-your-event/image-4.png)

### **Weekly Court Report**

The Weekly Court Report is emailed every Tuesday at 9 AM PT to opted-in users. Each report is personalized by location and may include:

- Venue announcements (e.g., court closures)
- Promoted sessions within 50 miles
- Public sessions with open spots in the next 8 days
- New open play opportunities

![image.png](/img/notion-help/how-dailypb-advertises-your-event/image-5.png)
