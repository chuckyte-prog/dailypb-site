# Manual vs Automatic Payment Comparison

<aside>
🛡️

Both [Manual](/docs/payments/manual-payments) and [Automatic Payments](/docs/payments/automatic-payments) enable all [Pro Features](/docs/hosts/pro-features-for-hosts) for sessions with signups and fees.

</aside>

## Manual Payments

**Benefits:**

- Instant access to funds through popular payment methods (Venmo, CashApp, Zelle, PayPal)
- Lower transaction fees - only 5% of the Event fee per signup (minimum $1)
- No Stripe account required
- Greater payment flexibility for participants
- Direct payment to host's preferred payment platform

**Considerations:**

- May need to follow up with participants for payment
- No automatic refund processing

## Automatic Payments

**Benefits:**

- Fully automated payment management
- Payment required for signup - no chasing payments
- Automatic refund processing for eligible cancellations
- Integrated with multiple payment methods (credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Secure payment processing using [Stripe](http://Stripe.com)

**Considerations:**

- Slightly higher fees ~ 8.1% + $0.31 per transaction
- Requires Stripe account setup

## Cost Comparison Example

For a $20 Event Fee:

| **Payment Type** | Player Total Cost | **Total Fees** | **Host Receives** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Manual Payment | $21.00 | $1.00 | $20 |
| Automatic Payment | $21.93 | $1.93 | $20 |

Notice in both examples the Host receives the full Event Fee.

<aside>
🚧 Note: Manual Payment fees are collected by the host and are then charged to the host at the end of the month, while Automatic Payment fees are taken out automatically at the time of payment.

</aside>
