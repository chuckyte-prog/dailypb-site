# My Stuff (My Calendar)

My Stuff (My Calendar) is available to Patron or higher tier levels.  

![Untitled](/img/notion-help/my-stuff-my-calendar/untitled.png)

My Stuff combines

1. All Sessions you are Attending 
2. All Sessions you on on the Waitlist for
3. All Sessions under an Event in which you are a Host
4. All Sessions with Group Privacy in which you are a Group Member
5. All Session with Group Privacy in which you are a Group Admin

* All combined searches include unlimited range.
