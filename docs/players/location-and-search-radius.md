# Location and Search Radius

These two things allow DailyPB to show you the Sessions that are local to you. 

You can view your current settings on the main Schedule page. 

# How to update your Location or Search Radius

1. Click on your Location or Search Radius to show the Location Preferences
    
    ![image.png](/img/notion-help/location-and-search-radius/image.png)
    
2. Enter any type of location (city or zip code) in the first field
3. If you want to update your Search Radius click the dropdown arrow and adjust. 

![image.png](/img/notion-help/location-and-search-radius/image-1.png)

1. Click Save.
You should see the new Location and Search Radius appear
