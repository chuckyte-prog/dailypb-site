# How to Become a Free Agent for a Session

*Learn how to make yourself available as a free agent so team captains can add you to their teams for a session.*

## Overview

When you want to play in a session but don't have a team yet, you can register as a free agent. This puts you in a pool of available players that team captains can see and choose from when building their teams. Becoming a free agent is a great way to get into sessions and meet new players.

## Before You Start

- Make sure you're viewing the session you want to play in

## Steps

### Step 1: Open the Teams View

From the session page, click the **Join or Create Team** button on the right side of the page.

![Session page showing the Join or Create Team button](/img/notion-help/how-to-become-a-free-agent-for-a-session/imported-image-j.jpg)

This will open the Teams modal where you can see all teams and available options.

### Step 2: Click Become Free Agent

In the Teams modal, you'll see some buttons at the top.

Click the **Become Free Agent** button.

![Teams modal with Become Free Agent button highlighted](/img/notion-help/how-to-become-a-free-agent-for-a-session/imported-image-sci-.jpg)

### Step 3: Fill Out Your Information

A form will appear asking for your details. Optional fields will be marked as such.

![Additional Info form with fields for phone, DUPR ID, skill level, and message](/img/notion-help/how-to-become-a-free-agent-for-a-session/imported-image-gcr.jpg)

### Step 4: Add Your Message (Optional)

In the "Free Agency Public Message" field, you can write something friendly and inviting. For example:

- "Choose me I'm so fun to play with!"
- "Looking for a competitive team!"
- "Happy to fill in wherever needed"

![Form showing example message being typed](/img/notion-help/how-to-become-a-free-agent-for-a-session/imported-image-sxoq.jpg)

### Step 5: Submit Your Free Agent Request

Once you've filled out all the information, click the **Become Free Agent** button at the bottom of the form.

### Step 6: Verify You're Listed

After submitting, you'll see yourself listed under the **Free Agents** tab. 

![Free Agents tab showing the newly added free agent](/img/notion-help/how-to-become-a-free-agent-for-a-session/imported-image-dio.jpg)

## Verify It Worked

You'll know you successfully became a free agent when:

- You appear in the Free Agents list
- The Free Agents tab shows a count (for example, "Free Agents (1)")

## What Happens Next

Once you're listed as a free agent:

- Team Captains will be notified via email that there a is a new free agent
- If a captain adds you to their team, you'll receive a notification
- You can edit your profile information at any time by clicking the edit icon (pencil) next to your name

![Edit profile view showing all your information](/img/notion-help/how-to-become-a-free-agent-for-a-session/imported-image-bpm.jpg)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I be a free agent for multiple sessions?**

Yes, you'll need to register as a free agent separately for each session you want to join.

**Can I remove myself from the free agent list?**

Yes, you can click the red minus button next to your name in the Free Agents list to remove yourself.

**What if no one picks me?**

If you're not selected for a team, you can try creating your own team or reaching out directly to session organizers for help getting placed.

**Can I edit my information after submitting?**

Yes, click the pencil (edit) icon next to your name in the Free Agents list to update your information or message.

**When a player is added to a team's bench, do they remain listed as a free agent?**

Yes. Adding a free agent to a team's bench does not remove them from free agency. They remain listed as a free agent until they are activated for a session.

**If a free agent is added to a team in one Session, does it affect their free agent status in another Session?**

No. Adding a free agent to a team in one Session has no effect on any other Session. It will not add them as a free agent elsewhere, and it will not remove them from free agency in another Session. Each Session's free agent list is independent.

**If a free agent is activated for a Session on one team, does it remove them from free agency on another Session?**

No. Activating a free agent for one Session only affects that Session. Their free agent status in any other Session remains unchanged.
