# How to Create a Team and Add Yourself to a Session

Learn how to create a team for a Team Session and add yourself as a player.

## Overview

When you want to play in a Team Session with friends or regular playing partners, you'll need to create a team and add yourself to it. This guide walks you through the complete process, from creating your team to getting yourself registered for the session.

Team Sessions are different from Open Play sessions. In Team Sessions, you play with pre-organized teams rather than being matched with other players.

## Before You Start

- Make sure you're signed in to your DailyPB account
- Find a Team Session you want to join (look for the **Team Event** label on session cards)
- Have your team name ready

## Steps

### Step 1: Find and Click on a Team Session

Start from your DailyPB home page where you can see all available sessions.

![step1-home-page.png](/img/notion-help/how-to-create-a-team-and-add-yourself-to-a-session/step1-home-page.png)

Look for sessions with the **Team Event** label. These are Team Sessions where you'll need to create or join a team. Click on the session you want to participate in and then click on Join or Create Team button.

### Step 2: Open the Teams Panel

Once you're on the session page, you'll see a **Create Team** button at the top of the Teams section.

![step2-teams-modal.png](/img/notion-help/how-to-create-a-team-and-add-yourself-to-a-session/68f9bcd0-a8c2-4692-b886-02e61a291fbc.png)

Click the **Create Team** button to start creating your team. You'll also see options for **Add My Team** (if you already have a team from a previous session) and **Become Free Agent** (if you want to be matched with others).

### Step 3: Enter Your Team Name

A "Create Team" dialog box will appear.

![step3-create-team-dialog.png](/img/notion-help/how-to-create-a-team-and-add-yourself-to-a-session/1e8d7d29-8d07-4f02-b921-70680bf10aa8.png)

Enter your team name in the **Team Name** field. Choose a name that's easy for you and your teammates to recognize. In this example, we're using "Kermit's Hermits."

After entering your team name, click the **Create Team** button at the bottom of the dialog.

### Step 4: Read the Important Instructions

After creating your team, you'll see an important message with instructions.

![step4-team-created-instructions.png](/img/notion-help/how-to-create-a-team-and-add-yourself-to-a-session/df57749f-2c8b-4870-9aff-c6c1e6b01640.png)

**This is very important:** Teams start out empty, and you must add at least one player (usually yourself) within 5 minutes, or the team will be automatically removed.

The instructions tell you:

1. **Add Yourself and Other Teammates** - Click "Join Team" to add yourself, or "Add Teammate" to add someone else. The first person to join becomes the Team Captain.
2. **Register Teammates for the Session** - Adding a player to the team doesn't automatically put them in the session. You need to take an additional step.

Click **Close** to continue.

### Step 5: Join Your Team

Look for your team name in the list. Click the **Join Team** button (shown as a button with your team name).

![step5-join-team-dialog.png](/img/notion-help/how-to-create-a-team-and-add-yourself-to-a-session/fda31d1d-da09-4092-ac45-eb0014810539.png)

A dialog will appear asking you to confirm joining the team. You can optionally enter:

- Your **Rating** (if you know your skill rating, typically between 2-8)
- Your **Skill Level** (select from Beginner to Advanced)

Click **Join Team** to add yourself to the team.

### Step 6: View Your Team with You Added

After joining, you'll see yourself listed on the team.

![step6-team-with-player.png](/img/notion-help/how-to-create-a-team-and-add-yourself-to-a-session/c260e2d2-383c-4523-ac48-e9095859b812.png)

You'll notice:

- Your name appears under **BENCH (1)**
- You're marked as **Captain** (because you were the first to join)
- Your status shows **Not Playing** in a dropdown

The blue information box at the top reminds you: "You have players on your team — now add them to the session."

### Step 7: Add Yourself to the Session

This is the final and crucial step. Being on the team doesn't mean you're registered for the session yet.

Click on the **Not Playing** dropdown next to your name.

![step7-player-added-to-session.png](/img/notion-help/how-to-create-a-team-and-add-yourself-to-a-session/8d6854a2-7238-4bd4-b679-754f551e1fe3.png)

From the dropdown menu, select **Add to Session**.

Once you do this, you'll see:

- Your status changes to **Playing**
- You move from the **BENCH** section to the **ACTIVE LINEUP** section
- The progress bar shows **1 / 4 Spots Filled**

The information box updates to show: "You have 3 spots remaining to fill."

## Verify It Worked

You'll know you successfully created your team and added yourself when:

- Your team name appears in the Teams list
- You see yourself in the **ACTIVE LINEUP** section
- Your status shows **Playing** in green
- The spots filled counter has increased (e.g., "1 / 4 Spots Filled")
- You're designated as **Captain**

## What Happens Next

Now that you've created your team and added yourself:

- You can add teammates by clicking the **Add Teammate** button
- Teammates can join your team by finding the session and clicking Request to Join
- Remember to activate each teammate to the session after they join the team (they'll also be on the bench initially)

## Common Issues

**Issue**: I created a team but it disappeared

**Solution**: Teams are automatically removed if no one joins within 5 minutes. After creating a team, make sure to immediately add yourself or at least one teammate. You’ll get an email if this happens.

**Issue**: I joined the team but I'm not playing

**Solution**: Joining the team puts you on the bench. You must also click the **Not Playing** dropdown and select **Add to Session** to actually register for the session.

**Issue**: My teammates can't find my team

**Solution**: Make sure they're looking at the same session. They should click on the Team Session, look in the Teams tab, and find your team by name. They can then click to join it.

**Issue**: What's the difference between "Bench" and "Active Lineup"?

**Solution**: The bench shows team members who have joined your team but haven't been activated to the session yet. Active lineup shows players who are actually registered to play in the session. Always move players from bench to active lineup.
