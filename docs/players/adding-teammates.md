# Adding Teammates

# How to Add Players to Your Session

Learn how to add teammates to your Session in DailyPB.

## Overview

As a Team Captain in DailyPB, you can add players to your team for an upcoming Session. This guide shows you how to add both existing DailyPB users and new players who don't yet have a DailyPB account. Session Hosts can also add players to teams using this same process.

## Before You Start

To add players to a Session, you need to be designated as a **Captain** of your team. If you're not yet a Captain, ask the Session Host to make you one, or they can add players directly.

## Adding a Player to Your Team

### Step 1: Open Your Session

Go to the Session page where you'd like to add players.

![01-session-page.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/01-session-page.jpg)

### Step 2: Click Join or Create Team

Click the **Join or Create Team** button on the right side of the page. This will open the Teams dialog.

![02-teams-dialog.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/02-teams-dialog.jpg)

You'll see your team listed with:

- How many spots are filled (e.g., "1 / 4 Spots Filled")
- Your current team members in the Active Lineup
- Players on the bench
- A blue **Next Step** box with instructions

### Step 3: Click Add Teammate

Click the **Add Teammate** button at the bottom of your team card.

This opens the "Add Player to Team" dialog where you can search for existing DailyPB users.

![03-add-teammate-dialog.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/03-add-teammate-dialog.jpg)

### Step 4: Search for a Player or Add a New User

You have two options:

**Option A: Add an Existing DailyPB User**

Enter the player's name in the search field. When they appear in the results, click on their name to add them.

**Option B: Add Someone New to DailyPB**

If the person you want to add doesn't have a DailyPB account yet, click **Add Non-DailyPB User to Team** to expand that section.

![04-add-non-dailypb-user.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/04-add-non-dailypb-user.jpg)

Fill in their information:

- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
- **Email**
- **Cell Phone**

Then click the **Create New User & Add to Team** button. They'll receive an email letting them know you signed them up.

## Adding Benched Players to Your Session

If you already have players on your team who are marked as "Not Playing" (benched), you can add them to the Session.

### Step 1: Find the Benched Player

In the Teams dialog, look in the **BENCH** section for players with a "Not Playing" status.

![05-benched-player.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/05-benched-player.jpg)

### Step 2: Click the Not Playing Dropdown

Click the dropdown arrow next to **Not Playing** for the player you want to add.

![06-player-actions-menu.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/06-player-actions-menu.jpg)

You'll see three options:

- **Become Captain** - Makes this player a team captain
- **Add to Session** - Adds the player to this Session
- **Remove from Team** - Removes the player from the team entirely

### Step 3: Click Add to Session

Click the **Add to Session** button.

![07-additional-info-dialog.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/07-additional-info-dialog.jpg)

A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to verify the player's cell phone number. Make sure the information is correct, then click the **Add to Session** button.

### Step 4: Verify They Were Added

![08-player-added-success.jpg](/img/notion-help/adding-teammates/08-player-added-success.jpg)

The player will now appear in your **ACTIVE LINEUP** section with a green "Playing" status. You'll also notice:

- Your "Spots Filled" count has increased
- The Team Rating may have updated
- The player is no longer in the Bench section

## Verify It Worked

You'll know you successfully added a player when they appear in the Active Lineup section with a green "Playing" status next to their name. The "Spots Filled" counter at the top of your team card will also increase.

## Common Questions

**Q: What if I can't find the Add Teammate button?**

You need to be a Team Captain to add players. Check with the Session Host to be made a Captain, or ask the Host to add players for you.

**Q: What happens when I add someone who doesn't have a DailyPB account?**

They'll receive an email notification that you've signed them up for the Session. They can then create their account and manage their profile.

**Q: Can I add players from the bench to multiple Sessions?**

Yes. Players on your team's bench can be added to any Session where your team is playing, as long as you're a Captain.

**Q: How do I remove someone I accidentally added?**

Click the dropdown next to their "Playing" status and select the appropriate option to change their status or remove them from the team.

**Q: Who can see the free agency list?**

Anyone can view the free agency list.

## What's Next

After adding players to your Session:

- Make sure all team members have confirmed their attendance
- Check that contact information is up to date
- Review your Team Rating to see how it's changed
