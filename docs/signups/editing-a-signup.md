# Editing a Signup

DailyPB makes it easy for Hosts to manage their events — including editing signups when information needs updating.

![chrome_pxrcVMrPVS.gif](/img/notion-help/editing-a-signup/chrome-pxrcvmrpvs.gif)

### 🔍 What is “Edit Signup”?

This feature allows **Session/Event Hosts as well as the Player themselves** to update key details about a Signup. This can help with:

- Correcting a typo in someone’s name or email
- Adding a phone number so a player can get notified of changes
- Updating skill level or DUPR rating to sort [Roster](/help/signups/roster-of-signups) more effectively

<aside>
🚧

Only Hosts and the Signup Player themselves can see private fields like email or phone number.

</aside>

---

### 📍 Where can I edit a Signup?

You can edit any Signup from two places:

1. **The Attendees Popup** (available when viewing your Session)

![Example of Editing a Signup from the Attendees Popup](/img/notion-help/editing-a-signup/chrome-ztzlog4sss.gif)

Example of Editing a Signup from the Attendees Popup

1. **The Host Page → Signups tab**

Click on a player's row or and a popup like this will appear:

![chrome_pxrcVMrPVS.gif](/img/notion-help/editing-a-signup/chrome-pxrcvmrpvs-1.gif)

---

### ✏️ What fields can I edit?

You can update the following:

- **Name**
- **Email**
- **Cell Phone**
- **Rating** (e.g., DUPR or general numeric)
- **Skill Level** (select from dropdown)

After making changes, **click the blue “Save” button** to apply them.

---

### 🔒 Who sees what?

- ✅ **Name is** public and will still appear publicly.
- 🔐 **Email, Phone Number, and any Skill Level or Rating** are private — only visible to the Host and the Signup player.

> 🧠 Players are not notified when you make changes, so if it's something sensitive, consider reaching out separately.
> 

---

### 🕒 When can I make edits?

You can edit a Signup **anytime — before or after the session**.
