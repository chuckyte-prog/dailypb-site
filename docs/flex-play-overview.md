# Flex Play Overview

# Flex Play: Flexible Game Creation for Dynamic Sessions

Flex Play gives you the freedom to create games on the fly without being locked into rigid, pre-determined matchups. Instead of committing every player to specific games upfront, you can build your session game-by-game, adapting to who's available, who's sitting out, and how the competition is flowing.

## Why Flex Play Matters

Traditional session formats require you to plan everything in advance—every matchup, every round, every player assignment. But pickleball sessions rarely go exactly as planned. Players arrive late, some need breaks, skill levels become apparent, and competitive balances shift. Flex Play adapts to reality instead of fighting against it.

## The Core Benefit: Build Games As You Go

With Flex Play enabled, you create each game when you're ready for it, not weeks in advance. The system shows you which players are available for the current round, lets you build balanced teams, and tracks everything automatically. You get the structure and standings tracking of organized play with the flexibility of pickup games.

## How It Works: Three Key Components

### Games Tab: Your Command Center

![Games tab showing round-based organization](/img/notion-help/flex-play-overview/imported-image-ine.jpg)

This is where you see all your games organized by round. Each round can have multiple games, and you can create new games whenever you need them. The interface shows you:

- **Progress tracking**: Visual indicators showing how many games are complete in each round
- **Quick game creation**: A "Create Game" button that opens the flexible matchup builder
- **Round filtering**: View all rounds together or focus on one round at a time
- **Player filtering**: See which players are involved in specific games

**The benefit:** You always know where you stand in the session and can create the next game without disrupting the flow of play.

### Game Creation: Smart Player Selection

![Game creation interface showing flexible team building](/img/notion-help/flex-play-overview/imported-image-asj.jpg)

![Smart warnings prevent scheduling conflicts](/img/notion-help/flex-play-overview/imported-image-pbv.jpg)

When you create a new game, Flex Play shows you exactly which players are available based on your settings. You can:

- **Toggle round-specific availability**: Choose whether to show only players who haven't played yet in this round, or include everyone
- **Build balanced teams**: Add or remove players from each side with simple click controls
- **See real-time conflicts**: Get warnings if you're trying to assign players who are already in games
- **Adjust on the fly**: Delete players from teams or trash the whole game setup if plans change

**The benefit:** No more scheduling conflicts or accidentally double-booking players. The system prevents mistakes and makes it easy to create fair matchups.

### Results Tab: Automatic Standings

![Live standings showing player rankings and statistics](/img/notion-help/flex-play-overview/imported-image-oeps.jpg)

Every game you create feeds into live standings that update instantly when scores are entered. The Results tab shows:

- **Live leaderboard**: Real-time rankings sorted by wins, losses, and point differentials
- **Comprehensive stats**: Win percentage, average point differential, total points scored/allowed, and games played
- **Customizable sorting**: Change how standings are calculated (primary and secondary sort criteria)
- **Exportable data**: Download standings for sharing or record-keeping

**The benefit:** Players can see exactly where they stand without you having to manually track anything. The competitive element stays front and center.

## Who Benefits Most from Flex Play

**League organizers** who want structure without rigidity—you can plan rounds without planning every individual matchup.

**Drop-in coordinators** who need to accommodate varying attendance—create games based on who actually shows up.

**Tournament directors** running ladder or king-of-the-court formats—flexibly match players while tracking performance.

**Recreational groups** that want standings without the hassle—get competitive tracking with pickup-game ease.

## The Bottom Line

Flex Play eliminates the tension between structure and flexibility. You get organized rounds, automatic standings, and performance tracking without having to predict the future or lock players into fixed schedules. Create games when you need them, adapt to changing circumstances, and let the system handle the record-keeping.
