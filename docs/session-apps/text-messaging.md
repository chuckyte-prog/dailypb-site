# Text Messaging

DailyPB allows hosts to send text messages to anyone in their session.  This feature is currently free.

# How to Send a Text Message

1. Navigate to the Session and click “Add / View Attendees” to pull up the Attendees list. 
    
    ![image.png](/img/notion-help/text-messaging/18975095-aeee-4835-aadd-66e6a5af10f8.png)
    
2. Scroll to the section titled “Session Apps” and click “Send Texts”
3. In the phone popup that will appear, click the plus icon in the top right corner to send a new message. 
4. Then click which group you want to message. All groups below 
    1. Attending - everyone who is signed up and not on the waitlist
    2. Paid - everyone who is Attending and marked as “paid”
    3. Unpaid - everyone who is Attending and marked as “unpaid”
    4. Waitlist - everyone who is on the Waitlis
    
    ![image.png](/img/notion-help/text-messaging/image.png)
    
5. Type your message in the box where is says “Type your message here…”

<aside>
💡

DailyPB automatically adds some helpful text to all messages sent including a direct Session link, your name, and your phone number in case they want to reply to you.  You can preview what the message will look like before you send it by clicking “Preview Message.”

</aside>

1. Once you are happy with your message, click “Send Text Message”
2. Your message will now appear in the sent messages area.
