# Creating Open Play Events

**DailyPB is the most powerful platform for managing Open Play**, giving hosts and venues complete control over their schedules, communication, and visibility. It’s built to simplify recurring sessions, automate updates, and connect the entire pickleball community through one seamless, intelligent system.

## 🌟 Benefits of DailyPB-Managed Open Play

DailyPB takes the work out of organizing Open Play and helps hosts, venues, and players stay in sync effortlessly. Here’s how:

- **Save time with automated scheduling** – Recurring sessions refresh automatically each week, so your Open Play calendar stays accurate without constant updates.
- **Enjoy full control and flexibility** – Cancel or delete individual sessions anytime without disrupting your entire schedule.
- **Collaborate seamlessly** – Add multiple hosts or custodians to share management duties and keep events running smoothly.
- **Keep communication easy and private** – Players can message hosts directly through DailyPB without ever seeing or sharing personal email addresses.
- **Keep everything in one place** – Manage all your Open Play events, updates, and communication from your Host Dashboard.
- **Keep your community informed** – Every public event appears instantly on the universal DailyPB calendar, making it easy for players nearby to discover and join.
- **Stay consistent across platforms** – Each Open Play session automatically syncs to its Venue’s calendar page, which has its own public web address for easy sharing and embedding.
- **Adapt to outdoor conditions** – Sessions can be set to end at sunset, helping venues maintain accurate daylight schedules automatically.

# FAQ

*Why do I only see 6 months of recurring open play Sessions?*

For recurring open play, DailyPB auto-generates six months of weekly sessions. At the five-month mark, it adds another six months, continuing indefinitely. 

*What if I update the time of the open play, when it regenerates, will it use the updated time?*

Yes. More specifically, it will use the most recent time of any auto-generated, non-deleted Session. 

*What if I have a recurring open play but I’ve deleted all the upcoming Sessions, will it continue to auto-generate?*

No. When you are about to delete the last Session for a recurring open play, the system will prompt you, letting you know that this will cancel future recurring Sessions.
