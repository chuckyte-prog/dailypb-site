# How to Change Event Image

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aGFn3RX6FLg](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aGFn3RX6FLg)

### How to Update Event Image

1. **Log In:**
    - Sign in to your DailyPB account.
2. **Navigate to Your Event:**
    - Go to "Host Dashboard" to easily find the event you want to update.
    - Click on it.
3. **Edit Event:**
    - Scroll down and click on "Global Event Tools."
    - Then Select "Edit Event."
4. **Change the Thumbnail:**
    - Click on the current thumbnail image.
    - Choose from existing images or upload a new one (recommended size: 1200x738 or 13:8 ratio).
5. **Upload and Save:**
    - Upload your selected image.
    - Click "Save and Close."
6. **Verify:**
    - Ensure the new image is displayed for your event.
