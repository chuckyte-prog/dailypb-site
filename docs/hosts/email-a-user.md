# Email a User

You can easily reach out to other players on DailyPB through their profiles.

### Step 1: Open the User’s Profile

- Click on a player’s name or profile image anywhere you see it (e.g., in a session list, leaderboard, or event page).
- This will open their **Profile Page**.

### Step 2: Click “Message User”

- On their profile, click the **Message User** button.
- A popup window will appear, allowing you to send a direct message.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/email-a-user/image.png)

### Step 3: Compose and Send Your Message

- If you’re logged in, your name and email will be prefilled automatically.
- Type your message in the text box, then click **Send**.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/email-a-user/image-1.png)

### Step 4: What Happens Next

- The recipient will receive an email containing:
    - **Your name**
    - **Your email address**
    - **Your message content**

They can then reply directly to your email to continue the conversation.
