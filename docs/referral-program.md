# Referral Program

## Earn by Sharing

Love DailyPB? Invite other hosts you know to join and earn rewards through our referral program! Here’s how it works.

## How to Get Started

1. **Grab Your Referral Link**
    - Log in to your DailyPB account and visit your profile.
    - Under the **"Referrals"** section, you’ll find your unique referral link. Copy it and share it with hosts you know or connect with in social groups who could use DailyPB.
    
    ![image.png](/img/notion-help/referral-program/image.png)
    
2. **They Sign Up, You Earn**
    - When a new host uses your link to sign up for a DailyPB account, you’ll be saved as their referrer.
    - You’ll get an **email confirmation** once they’ve joined.
    - You’ll earn **10% of the signup fees paid by their players**—every time a player signs up through them, forever!
3. **Get Paid Monthly**
    - We process payments monthly via Venmo (for now).
    - You’ll receive your earnings as long as you’ve made at least **$10** that month. If you’re below $10, your earnings roll over to the next month.
4. **Track Your Earnings**
    - See your progress anytime in your profile under **"Referrals"**.
    - Check how much you’ve earned and how many hosts you’ve referred.

## Rules to Follow

- **New Signups Only**: Referrals apply only to hosts who are new to DailyPB.
- **No Spam**: Share your link only with people you know personally or are part of a social group with. Spamming is not allowed.
- **Unlimited Potential**: There’s no cap on how many hosts you can refer or how much you can earn!

## Why Refer Hosts to DailyPB?

- They get a platform to manage players, and you earn passive income for every player they bring in—indefinitely.
- The more hosts you invite, the more you can make!

## FAQ

***What happens if I send someone my referral link, but they sign up later or from a different page? Will I still get credit?***

Yes! When you share your referral link, DailyPB doesn’t just rely on the referral code in the URL. It also sets a cookie with your referral code on the user’s device. When they sign up—even later or from a different page—DailyPB checks for that cookie to credit you as their referrer.

This only fails if they sign up from a different device where the cookie isn’t present and without using your link. If you think this has happened, contact us with the referred user’s details and a screenshot as proof, and we’ll sort it out.
