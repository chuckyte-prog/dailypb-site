### Why Set Up a Group Page

Creating a **Group Page** gives your pickleball community a home base. Whether you’re a club, ambassador, or just a local crew that plays weekly, your group page keeps everyone connected, informed, and growing together.

Groups make it easier to:

- Showcase all your **sessions** in one place.
- Help players **find your group** and join with a single click.
- Allow **private Groups** a secure way to connect
- Reduce the clutter of texts, spreadsheets, or multiple chat threads.

### Main Benefits

### 🗓️ All Sessions in One Place

Once your Group is linked to your Sessions, every Session under those events automatically appears on your Group Page. This makes it easy for members to see upcoming play opportunities without searching across the site.

### 🔒 Privacy Control

You can decide whether your group is **Public** or **Private**:

- **Public Groups** — Anyone on DailyPB can find and view your group page.
- **Private Groups** — Only approved members can see Sessions and details.

### 💬 Community Connections

Groups can include links to WhatsApp, Facebook, or other community chats so players can coordinate, share photos, or plan outside events.

### 🧭 Centralized Organization

Each group serves as your base of operations — managing announcements, signups, and communications in one easy dashboard.

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Build Membership

Players can request to join, receive automated welcome emails, and immediately start seeing your Group’s Sessions and updates.

---

### 🔗 How to Get Started

1. Login and go to your [Host Dashboard](https://dailypb.com/host).
2. Click **Groups** and select **+ New Group.**
3. Add your group’s image, short description, and privacy settings. View all [Settings.](/docs/groups/group-settings)
4. Connect your Group to existing Sessions - they will magically appear on your Group Calendar.
5. Share your custom link (example: `dailypb.com/group/yourname`) with your community.

### How to Link a Group to a Venue

<aside>
💡

Only public Groups that are linked to the Venue will show up on a Venue page

</aside>

![unnamed (12).jpg](/img/notion-help/basic-group-info/unnamed-12.jpg)

1. Open the Edit Group screen either through the [Host Dashboard](http://dailypb.com/host?view=group) or from the link on the Group page itself
2. In the General Tab find the field named “primary venue.”
3. Enter the name of the Venue in that field
4. Click Save

You should now be able to navigate to the Venue and view your Group at the top.
