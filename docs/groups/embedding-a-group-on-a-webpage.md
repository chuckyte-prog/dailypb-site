# Embedding a Group on a Webpage

---

# How to Embed Your Group Page on Your Website

Embedding your DailyPB Group page makes it easy for players to see your sessions directly on your own website.

---

## Step 1: Go to Your Host Dashboard

1. Visit [**dailypb.com/host**](https://dailypb.com/host). (make sure you are logged in first)
2. Click **Groups** in the left menu.

---

## Step 2: Select the Group You Want to Embed

- You’ll see a list of all your Groups.
- Click on the **Group name** that you want to embed.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/embedding-a-group-on-a-webpage/image.png)

---

## Step 3: Click the Embed Button

- Inside the Group management page, look for the **Embed** button in the header.
- Click it to reveal the embed code.

---

## Step 4: Copy the Embed Code

1. A popup box will appear with an HTML `<iframe>` code snippet.
2. Click **Copy Code** to copy the embed code to your clipboard.

![image.png](/img/notion-help/embedding-a-group-on-a-webpage/image-1.png)

---

## Step 5: Paste Into Your Website

- Open your website editor (WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, etc.).
- Edit the page where you want the schedule to appear.
- Switch to the **HTML / Code editor** view.
- Paste the embed code.
- Save or publish your changes.

---

## Step 6: Test the Embed

- Visit your website page.
- You should now see your DailyPB Group schedule displayed live.
    - Note that the page will be formatted so that the DailyPB header and your Group’s main image will not be displayed.
- Click a session: it will open within a popup on your Group page on DailyPB.com

---

## Tips

- The embed is responsive and resizes to fit your content.
- Partner websites can also embed your Group page to promote your sessions.
