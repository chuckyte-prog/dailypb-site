# How to Download the Spreadsheet

1. Open the Attendees Popup by clicking the Session, then clicking “View / Add Attendees.”
2. Under the list of Attendees you will find a button “Download CSV” 

![image.png](/img/notion-help/spreadsheet-of-signups/image.png)

1. Your computer or mobile device will automatically download the spreadsheet.
