# How to Add Alternative Payment Methods to an Event

**Welcome to DailyPB's Payment Method Setup Guide**

At DailyPB, we understand the importance of flexibility when it comes to payment methods for hosting events. To accommodate various preferences, we support four alternative payment methods: Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, and Paypal. Here's how you can activate and manage these payment methods as a host:

**Activating Payment Methods for Events:**

1. **Selecting Payment Methods:** When adding or editing an event, hosts can choose the payment methods they will accept. Look for the "Types of payment you will accept" section, which features a multi-select dropdown menu.
2. **Choosing Payment Methods:** In the dropdown menu, hosts can select one or more payment methods from Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, and Paypal.
    
    ![Alternative payment method options (Add/Edit Event screen)](How%20to%20Add%20Alternative%20Payment%20Methods%20to%20an%20Event/Untitled.png)
    
    Alternative payment method options (Add/Edit Event screen)
    
3. **Completing Payment Method Forms:** After selecting a payment method, a form field will appear for you to enter the ID for that payment type.

![Enter the appropriate ID. Use **help** function and **test** functionality before saving.](How%20to%20Add%20Alternative%20Payment%20Methods%20to%20an%20Event/Untitled%201.png)

Enter the appropriate ID. Use **help** function and **test** functionality before saving.

**Optional: Managing Payment Method IDs:**

1. **Saving Payment Method IDs:** Hosts can save their payment method IDs in the Monetization → Manual Payments section of their account.  This step streamlines the process of adding payment methods to events.
2. **Automatic ID Population:** Once payment method IDs are saved in Manual Payments section, they will automatically populate when adding a new payment method to an event. This feature saves time for hosts by eliminating the need to re-enter IDs manually.
3. **Note on ID Updates:** It's important to note that changing a payment method ID in the Financial Settings page will not automatically update the corresponding ID on previously created events. Hosts must manually update payment method IDs for existing events if necessary.

![image.png](How%20to%20Add%20Alternative%20Payment%20Methods%20to%20an%20Event/image.png)

With these simple steps, hosts can easily activate and manage payment methods for their events on DailyPB. If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team for help.
