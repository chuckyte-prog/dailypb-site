# Skill Level / Rating Requirements

Hosts can require players to provide their Skill Level or Rating during signup, ensuring players are grouped with others of similar skill levels. 

![image.png](Skill%20Level%20Rating%20Requirements/image.png)

If enabled this information will be required for all Signups and is available to Hosts on the Attendees Popup, [Printable Roster,](Roster%20of%20Signups%2017d4710b52ff80919687d0772208cac4.md) downloadable spreadsheet (CSV), and Round Robin tool.

# How to Enable Skill Requirements on an Event

1. Open the Event Edit menu by navigating to the Session, clicking on “Global Event Tools,” then clicking on “Edit Event.”
2. Navigate to the 4th page of the menu
3. Click on “Player must enter skill” in the “Skill level requirement to sign up” section. 

![image.png](Skill%20Level%20Rating%20Requirements/image%201.png)

1. Choose the type of skill level: Rating or Recreational Level
    1. Rating: numeric (example: 3.7)
    2. Recreational Level (example: Advanced Beginner)
    

![image.png](Skill%20Level%20Rating%20Requirements/image%202.png)

1. Click “Save & Close”
