# Custom Fields

Collect additional information from each signup using up to three custom fields.

**Example:** *"What is your team name?"*

Responses are included in the signup confirmation email, visible on the Attendees screen, and downloadable as a spreadsheet (CSV).

![image.png](Custom%20Fields/image.png)

# How to add Custom Fields

1. Navigate to the Session, scroll to Event Tools and click on “Global Event Settings,” then click “Edit Event”
2. Navigate to page 4 of the Edit Event menu and find the area listed as “Custom Form Fields”
3. Enter your question in any of the custom fields. 
4. Click “Save & Close”

Now, when someone clicks to join a Session or Waitlist, they will be prompted with these questions. Providing responses is optional.

All responses are only visible to Hosts.
