# Refunds

Refunds only apply to Sessions using [Automatic Payments](Automatic%20Payments%201534710b52ff80a1a3c2cdd2a27d8aaf.md). For [Manual Payments](Manual%20Payments%201534710b52ff80549bcce132eb21089e.md), the Host is responsible for issuing refunds through their preferred payment method.

# General Info

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the session.
- The refund amount equals the total paid minus the Stripe processing fee and the DailyPB fee.
- Refunds are deducted from the Main Host’s Stripe account. If there are insufficient funds, the connected bank account or debit card will be charged.
- Attendees who cancel before the Refund Deadline receive an automatic refund.
    - If they cancel after the Refund Deadline, they are notified that no refund will be issued.
    - This Refund Deadline is clearly displayed before attendees sign up and pay.

# How to Issue a Refund to an Attendee

1. Open the Attendees Popup by clicking “View / Add Attendees” in the Session details screen
2. Click the dropdown to view more info next to the Attendee you wish to Refund
3. Click the “Refund” button and confirm the Refund.

Both the Payee and Payer for the original Transaction are emailed regarding the refund.

# How to Issue a Refund After an Attendee was Removed from Session

1. Open the Host page and click on [Signups](http://dailypb.com/host?view=signups)
2. Find the Signup you wish to Refund
3. Click on the Money Icon to bring up the Transaction Details

4. Click the “Refund” button and confirm

# How to Issue a Refund to an Attendee after a Session was Deleted

1. Open the [Financial Dashboard](http://dailypb.com/host)
2. Check the box where it says “Deleted Sessions” to show all Transactions whose Sessions were deleted. 

![image.png](Refunds/image%202.png)

1. Open the Transaction Details popup by clicking anywhere on the line item that shows the removed Attendee
2. Click the “Refund” button and confirm

# FAQ

*How do refunds work and do I need to manually process them if a player cancels?* 

Every Session has a Refund Deadline (default 48 hours) the Host can set. If a Player cancels before that deadline they will automatically get a refund. However, as the host you can manually issue them a refund at any time.
