# Sign In Help / Troubleshooting

*Here are some troubleshooting steps if you're having trouble logging in to DailyPB. They are listed in order of most likely to solve your issue.*

1. Refresh your browser and try again—sometimes the site updates and needs a page refresh. This can also help if the site isn't responding.
2. If you signed up with Google, make sure to use the Google Sign-in service.
3. If you accessed DailyPB through a Facebook link, you won't be able to log in with Google (Facebook and Google aren't compatible). Instead, open a regular browser, go to [dailypb.com](http://dailypb.com), and sign in with Google there.
4. If you can't remember your account details—like which email you used or whether you signed up with Google—try entering different email addresses in the Email field to see what messages appear.
    1. If your email isn't in the DailyPB database, you'll get an error and won't be able to proceed. Try other email addresses you might have used to sign up.
    2. If you originally signed up with Google but are trying to use DailyPB's regular sign-in, you'll get this error and won't be able to continue.
