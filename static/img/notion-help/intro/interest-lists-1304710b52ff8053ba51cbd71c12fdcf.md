# What are Interest Lists

An Interest List is a tool for Hosts to manage and track Users interested in their Events. Users can join an Interest List through a linked Event/Session or a dedicated Interest List webpage, making it easy for hosts to communicate with potential participants and share updates.

Hosts can customize their Interest Lists by naming them, adding a description, creating custom sign-up fields, and assigning admins to help manage them. 

The Interest List webpage can be shared via a unique link or QR code, and users can sign up directly or during event registration.

Features include:

- Managing subscribers: add, edit, delete, and export user information.
- Linking Interest Lists to Events: allow users to sign up even if they cannot attend a Session
- Sharing via a customizable website or downloadable QR code for easy access.

Interest Lists streamline communication and organization, enabling hosts to build strong connections with their audience.

# Creating an Interest List

1. Once logged in navigate to [DailyPB.com/host](http://DailyPB.com/host)
2. Click on **Interest Lists**
3. Click **New**
4. Fill in the fields with your info
    1. List name - this is the public name of your list
    2. URL Slug - this will be part of the website DailyPB makes for your Interest List. For example if you’re making an Interest List for Coached Play with Chuck, then you could type in coached-play-chuck in this field. Your website will become dailypb.com/interest/coached-play-chuck. 
    3. Main Image - this image will display on your Interest List website
    4. Description - this will display on your Interest List website
    5. Custom form fields (1-3) - You can enter questions you want people to answer when they sign up to your Interest List. They will not be required. 
    6. Admins - You can add other DailyPB users you would like to help manage the Interest List
5. Click **Create List**

At this point you will get an email confirming your new Interest List as well as a link to your Interest List Website. Note: URL slugs need to be unique and can only use certain characters so the website address may be slightly different from what you entered in the URL Slug field. You can update that field anytime to get a website URL that you like. 

# Your Interest List Website

# Linking an Interest List to an Event

When editing an Event follow these steps.

![image.png](Interest%20Lists/28fa7624-6646-42b9-89b9-e2af6b491f4f.png)

1. Click on Screen **2** to view the visibility and signup settings
2. Check the box **Enable Interest List Signup**
3. Your interest list(s), or any previously linked to the Event, will appear underneath the checkbox. Select one. If you don’t have one already created, there will be a button that automatically creates one for you.
4. Click **Save & Close**

Your Session popup will now show some special text in the sign up area. If the user viewing your Session is not already a subscriber of the linked Interest List, they will see some text such as **Can’t attend? Get notified of future events.**

If someone viewing the Session is on the Interest List then the text will change to something like **You’re on the interest list. Click here for details.**

![image.png](Interest%20Lists/image%203.png)

If the User clicks that link they will be taken to your Interest List website. 

Additionally, when someone goes to sign up for your Session, the final screen before they confirm their participation will have a checkbox to join the Interest List. That checkbox is checked by default.

# Managing Interest List Subscribers

You can manage all subscribers by selecting the appropriate Interest List on DailyPB.com/host.

On that page you can: add subscribers manually, delete subscribers, view and edit individual subscriber information, and export subscribers to CSV or to your clipboard. 

## Adding a Subscriber

Manually add a subscriber by clicking the **Add Subscriber** button. You can then search by first or last name. 

Click **Add** next to their name to add them to your currently selected Interest List.

![image.png](Interest%20Lists/image%205.png)

<aside>
🚧

If a subscriber is not in the current Interest List, but in another one of your Interest Lists, there will be a warning given. It will still allow you to add them.

</aside>

![image.png](Interest%20Lists/image%206.png)

## Modifying and Deleting a Subscriber

To modify a subscriber, find them in your list of subscribers and then click **Edit** next to their name. (You can enter their name in the filter box to find them quickly). This will bring up an Edit Subscriber popup window where you can view and edit their info.

The Notes field is a private field that only Interest List Admins can view. 

When you are done editing the subscriber click **Edit Subscriber** to save your changes. 

You can also delete this subscriber by clicking the **Delete Subscriber** button. 

![image.png](Interest%20Lists/image%207.png)

## Exporting Subscribers

You can export your list of subscribers in the Tools section.

![image.png](Interest%20Lists/image%208.png)

1. Copy All Names - this will copy all names to your clipboard. 
2. Copy All Emails - this will copy all emails to your clipboard. 
3. Download All to CSV - this will download a CSV formatted spreadsheet to your device with **all** subscriber information including the Notes field.

<aside>
💡

Pro Tip: If you want to email all subscribers, click **Copy All Emails** and then go to your email program and simply paste them in. Most people will paste in the BCC section so that subscribers will not see the full list of emails.

</aside>
