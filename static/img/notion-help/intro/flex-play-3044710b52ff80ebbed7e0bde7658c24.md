# Flex Play: Flexible Game Creation for Dynamic Sessions

Flex Play gives you the freedom to create games on the fly without being locked into rigid, pre-determined matchups. Instead of committing every player to specific games upfront, you can build your session game-by-game, adapting to who's available, who's sitting out, and how the competition is flowing.

## Why Flex Play Matters

Traditional session formats require you to plan everything in advance—every matchup, every round, every player assignment. But pickleball sessions rarely go exactly as planned. Players arrive late, some need breaks, skill levels become apparent, and competitive balances shift. Flex Play adapts to reality instead of fighting against it.
