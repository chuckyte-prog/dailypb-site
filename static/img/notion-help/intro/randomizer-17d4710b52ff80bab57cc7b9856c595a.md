# Randomizer

This tools simply takes all Attendees and randomizes the order.

![chrome_1tjmIMcOGZ.gif](Randomizer/chrome_1tjmIMcOGZ.gif)

### How to Use this App

1. Click the app from the Attendees screen to load it into a new page
2. Click “Randomize” to reorder the list

### Use Cases in Pickleball

1. **Court Assignments** – Randomly assign players to courts to ensure balanced and diverse matchups during open play or round-robin sessions.
2. **Partner Pairings** – Create random doubles pairings to encourage social interaction and challenge players to adapt to different teammates.
3. **King of the Court Format** – Rotate players between courts in an unpredictable manner, determining who moves up, down, or sits out after each round.
4. **Social Mixers** – Mix up players in a fun, low-pressure way to help participants meet new people and enjoy varied gameplay.
5. **Prize or Giveaway Selection** – Randomly pick winners for event prizes or giveaways, adding an exciting element to your session.
6. **Drill Rotations** – Randomly assign players to drills or stations during a coaching session or clinic to keep the experience fresh and engaging.
7. **Tiebreaker Situations** – Use the tool to randomly determine which players or teams advance in tournaments when ties need to be broken.
8. **Game Format Determination** – Decide on game formats, such as doubles or singles, by randomizing attendees for a unique and engaging session setup.
