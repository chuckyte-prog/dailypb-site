# The Wheel

![chrome_ED8eAIhAPe.gif](The%20Wheel/chrome_ED8eAIhAPe.gif)

The **Wheel App** is a versatile tool that randomly selects one attendee, often for removal or reassignment. 

### How to Use The Wheel

1. Click the app from the Attendees screen to load it into a new page

1. Click “Spin” to spin the wheel. You will see a victims name appear.

![image.png](The%20Wheel/image.png)

Once the Wheel selects a "victim," they are removed from the list and cannot be chosen again.

### Use Cases in Pickleball

1. King of the Court (see callout below)
2. **Drill Rotations:** Select players at random for specific drills or to lead certain warm-up exercises.
3. **Prize Drawings:** Use the wheel to pick winners for giveaways or prizes at events.

<aside>
💡

King of the Court Use Case:
This app was originally developed for a **King of the Court** format where 17 or 18 players competed for 16 spots across 4 courts. When a player lost on the lowest court, they sat out; if they lost again, the wheel was spun to randomly swap them with another player. This gave rise to its original name, "The Wheel of Death," as the chosen player had to sit out and start over on the lowest court.
