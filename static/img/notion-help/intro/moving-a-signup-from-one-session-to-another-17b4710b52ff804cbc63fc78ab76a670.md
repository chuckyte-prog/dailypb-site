# Moving a Signup from one Session to Another

<aside>
💡

If you need to cancel and reschedule a Session, consider simply [changing the date of the Session.](Changing%20the%20Time%20or%20Date%20of%20a%20Session%2018c4710b52ff808885cbfff8373b09a8.md)  This way, you can keep all your signups intact.

</aside>

As a host, you can transfer players from one session to another, even if they have already paid. Follow these steps to complete the transfer:

### Step 1: Record Player Information

Before removing the player, write down any provided details such as their email and cell phone number. You’ll need this information later.

### Step 2: Remove the Player from the Original Session

1. Open the **Attendees Popup** in the original session.
2. Click the dropdown arrow next to the player’s name.
3. Select the trash can icon and confirm the removal.

![After clicking the dropdown arrow you will see the Trash Can icon appear. ](Moving%20a%20Signup%20from%20one%20Session%20to%20Another/image.png)

After clicking the dropdown arrow you will see the Trash Can icon appear. 

**Note:** Both the player and the host(s) will receive an email confirming the removal.

### Step 3: Add the Player to the New Session

1. Navigate to the new session and open the **Attendees Popup**.
2. Scroll to the "Manually Add Attendee" section.
3. Enter the player’s email to locate their account and click the **Add** button.
    
    ![In the Manually Add Attendee section you can enter the Players name or email to find them. Then you can click the Add button.](Moving%20a%20Signup%20from%20one%20Session%20to%20Another/image%201.png)
    
    In the Manually Add Attendee section you can enter the Players name or email to find them. Then you can click the Add button.
    
4. Input their cell phone number and any additional details as needed.

Note: Both the player and the host(s) will receive an email confirming the new Signup.

### Step 4: Update Payment Status

1. In the new session’s **Attendees Popup**, locate the player you just added.
2. Click the dropdown arrow next to their name.
3. Select **Mark as Paid** to transfer their payment status.

![image.png](Moving%20a%20Signup%20from%20one%20Session%20to%20Another/image%202.png)

By following these steps, you can seamlessly transfer players between sessions while ensuring all necessary details are updated.
