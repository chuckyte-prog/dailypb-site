# Roster of Signups

You can generate a printable Roster for taking with you on the day of your Session. Use it for checkins as well as assigning players to courts based on level.

# How to Generate a Roster

1. Open the Attendees Popup by navigating to your Session and clicking on “View / Add Attendees” or the number attending

1. Click on the “Roster” button below the list of Attendees. 

This will open a new window with a Roster formatted for printing. 

It will not look good on a mobile phone browser.

![image.png](Roster%20of%20Signups/image.png)

1. Sort the Roster by name or skill level/rating. 

(Note: you will be able to sort by Skill Level or Rating if you have enabled that in your Event Settings.)

![image.png](Roster%20of%20Signups/image%201.png)

1. Print the Roster by using your computers print function.
